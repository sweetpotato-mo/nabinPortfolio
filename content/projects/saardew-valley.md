7-week team project (6 members) covering the full software development lifecycle of an organic farming simulation named **“Saardew Valley”**, structured to mirror a professional software engineering process.

- Architected the simulation using PlantUML to produce comprehensive UML Class, State, and Sequence diagrams for entities, time, and farm resources.
- Implemented core logic in Kotlin, applying established design patterns to manage resources such as time, the farm map, and crop/livestock state.
- Drove quality engineering practices, achieving approximately **93–97% code coverage** across core modules with JUnit 5 and Kotest tests, including mocks with Mockito/Kotest.
- Worked within two clearly separated phases (requirements & architecture, then implementation & testing), reflecting how real-world teams iterate on a shared codebase.
