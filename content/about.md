## About Me

I'm Nabin, a B.Sc. Computer Science student at Universität des Saarlandes in Saarbrücken, with a minor in Computational Linguistics. Originally from Kathmandu, Nepal, I came to Germany through the VSI MINT preparatory programme and have been here since 2023.

My interests span low-level programming, software engineering, and algorithm design. I enjoy problems that require thinking carefully about efficiency and correctness, whether that's managing memory in C, designing a clean class hierarchy, or writing tests that actually catch bugs.

I previously worked as a teacher in Nepal, which gave me a strong foundation in breaking down complex topics clearly – a habit that carries over into how I write code and documentation.

I'm currently looking for a working student (Werkstudent) position or internship in software development in Saarbrücken and the surrounding region, where I can contribute to a real codebase alongside a professional team while continuing my studies.
